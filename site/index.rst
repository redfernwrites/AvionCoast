Avion Coast Campaign Documentation
==================================

Welcome the the Avion Coast's documentation page! The Avion Coast is a homebrewed Dungeons and Dragons (DnD) campaign setting being used for a personal (not recorded, not streamed) long-term game played by a group of friends, which started in July 2025. The last session held was on |last_session|.

This site can be treated as a collection of lore gathered by the party throughout the campaign, and is specificially intended to serve as a reference for the party members, and the Dungeon Master (DM) who runs the game. 

What you can find information about on this site:
-------------------------------------------------
- The campaign setting (generally)
- NPCs the party has encountered
- Specific places the party has visited
- Homebrewed items, spells, and classes that appear in the campaign
- House rules used during this campaign
- What happened in past sessions
- Anything else the DM feels is worthy of posting

What you will *not* find information about on this site:
--------------------------------------------------------
- General DnD information - if it exists in a book, reference will be made to the information's location
- Things that has not been revealed to *all* the party members
- The Big Bag Evil Guy (BBEG), antagonists, villains, etc. and their yet-to-be-revealed plans, secrets, and lairs
- Specific stat blocks for homebrewed monsters

This site is published through GitHub, and if you're a party member, you can `contribute to the repo <https://github.com/redfernwrites/AvionCoast>`_!
